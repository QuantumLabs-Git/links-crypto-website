üìñ The History of Links

Links was one of the more beloved Microsoft Office Assistant characters, especially among cat lovers who found it a much more appealing alternative to Clippy!

First downloadable for Office 97 and included in Microsoft Office 2000 & beyond, Links took the form of a small light brown cat with a whiter underbelly, a blue collar, and red inner ears.

What made Links particularly endearing was how authentically cat-like the animations were. Whoever designed Links obviously had real-life cat experience.

  • Using his paw print as a stamp of approval

Links even needed reading glasses (dark blue with "Old Granny" style chains) and had dark paws that served as his signature.
